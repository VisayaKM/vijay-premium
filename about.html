About Vijay's Biryani Food House

Vijay replies to nearly every review himself. Scroll through hundreds of them and that's the pattern that stands out, not the exception: guests describing a welcome that feels like family before the food even arrives.

The kitchen brings together Indian and Arabic cooking on one menu — biryani and tandoori alongside kapsa and shawarma-style plates — and guests keep coming back for both sides of it. The beef biryani and the shawarma each earn their own separate mentions across dozens of reviews, and it's common to see the same names show up more than once.

Right in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, that combination of warm service and food people specifically travel back for has built a 4.9-star rating from 566 Google reviews. Whether it's dine-in, curbside pickup, or delivery, the same hospitality is the point.
